WHO WE ARE

Bountiful Foods will have as its main purpose of helping people to lead a healthy life, helping people to eat healthy, as well as allowing people to learn about the local foods in the area. We provide users with an option to take care of their health through a healthy diet of fresh and healthy foods. You will have the opportunity to interact with the website and customize your own drinks so that you can eat healthy, comfortably, without leaving home. Those who are or farmers in the area who wish to be suppliers of the company, can get in touch with it, and learn about the history and corporate purpose, form commercial ties.

Our History

We are a company recently founded in 2010, and we seek to help people lead a healthy life. Bountiful Foods is a small business that specializes in locally grown, organic foods. They are physically located in Carlsbad, California, however they have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. The company sells fruits and products directly from farmers in the area, but also uses some national and international suppliers.

About Us

The company has people trained to be able to carry out a business of this magnitude and help farmers to be our suppliers in a reliable and safe way. The owner-operated business caters to the traffic at its store in Carlsbad and, of course, its food trucks. They deliver and fulfill those orders over the phone and through the website.

Healthy

The mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, freshly prepared, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and tourists visiting the coastal areas. The theme should support healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience. In this way, people will be able to identify with our company and at the same time will be able to lead a healthier lifestyle.
