Target Audience

We have in our target, audience who are meteorologists and weather presenters, who need a reliable source to share weather information to their audience. We also target individuals who need to understand the weather for any location at any given time

We are technologically driven meaning a majority segment of our market are going to be those who are very familiar with the internet.
